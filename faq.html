FAQ
Do I need a visa to travel to India?
Yes. You will need to apply for a tourist visa, also known as an e-visa, before traveling to India.
You can apply for an e-visa at the Indian government website.
What is the weather in Goa in November?
Average maximum day time temperatures are at a warm 32ºC (90ºF) during November, and the evening temperatures are pleasant, averaging around 24ºC (75ºC).

What should I know about traveling around in India?
English is widely spoken in major cities. We will arrange ground transportation between the Jaipur airport and the hotel, but for those who are traveling elsewhere in India, we recommend hiring a car (see below for recommendations) or taxi to get around. Uber is also a great option in many big cities. You will need to use Indian currency (rupees) when traveling through India. We recommend keeping cash on hand at all times, since credit cards are not widely accepted outside of major retailers (like hotels and big department stores). There are currency exchange bureaus at the airports and in most major cities, where you can exchange U.S. dollars for Indian rupees. Many places in India, including smaller shops, have begun to accept Google Pay and Apple Pay.
