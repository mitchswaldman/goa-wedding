Flying to Goa
We strongly recommend flying into the Goa Dabolim International Airport as it is much closer to the wedding venue. The other airport is on the north side of Goa, so it will take much longer to get to Beleza from there.

We will be coordinating ground transportation between the airport and the hotel. For your flight to Goa, we strongly recommend flying into the Goa Dabolim International Airport as it is much closer to the wedding venue.
